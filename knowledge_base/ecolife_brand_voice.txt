# EcoLife Brand Voice Guide

## Company Overview
EcoLife is a sustainability-focused company committed to environmental protection and green living.

## Brand Personality
- Passionate about sustainability
- Authentic
- Community-driven
- Educational
- Hopeful

## Tone of Voice
- **Warm and inspiring**: Create emotional connections through storytelling
- **Authentic**: Be genuine and transparent about challenges and wins
- **Empowering**: Show how individuals can make a difference
- **Educational**: Share practical tips and scientific facts

## Writing Guidelines

### Do:
- Use storytelling to illustrate impact
- Include personal anecdotes
- Share concrete actions people can take
- Use warm, inclusive language
- Celebrate small wins and progress

### Don't:
- Guilt-trip or shame readers
- Be preachy or judgmental
- Use doom-and-gloom language exclusively
- Oversimplify complex environmental issues
- Make it seem impossible to make a difference

## Hashtag Strategy
Always include 4-6 relevant hashtags:
- #Sustainability
- #EcoFriendly
- #GreenLiving
- #ClimateAction
- #SustainableFuture
- #ZeroWaste

## Post Structure
1. **Story/Hook** (1-2 sentences): Start with a relatable story or question
2. **Problem/Context** (2 sentences): Explain the environmental issue
3. **Solution/Action** (2-3 sentences): Share practical steps or solutions
4. **Impact** (1-2 sentences): Highlight the positive change possible
5. **Call-to-Action** (1 sentence): Invite community participation

## Example Phrases
- "Small changes, big impact..."
- "Together, we can..."
- "Every choice matters..."
- "Join us in making a difference..."
- "Here's how you can help..."
