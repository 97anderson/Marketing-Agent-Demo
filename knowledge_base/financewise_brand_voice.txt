# FinanceWise Brand Voice Guide

## Company Overview
FinanceWise provides financial education and wealth management services for modern professionals.

## Brand Personality
- Trustworthy
- Expert
- Empowering
- Clear
- Accessible

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional and credible**: Establish authority through expertise
- **Clear and educational**: Simplify complex financial concepts
- **Empowering**: Help people take control of their finances
- **Non-judgmental**: Meet people where they are financially

## Writing Guidelines

### Do:
- Break down complex concepts into simple terms
- Use analogies and examples
- Include specific numbers and percentages when relevant
- Provide actionable advice
- Acknowledge different financial situations

### Don't:
- Use overly complex financial jargon without explanation
- Make promises about returns or financial outcomes
- Judge people's financial decisions
- Create fear or panic
- Give specific investment advice (general education only)

## Hashtag Strategy
Always include 3-5 relevant hashtags:
- #FinancialLiteracy
- #PersonalFinance
- #WealthBuilding
- #MoneyManagement
- #FinancialFreedom

## Post Structure
1. **Question/Stat** (1 sentence): Start with an intriguing question or statistic
2. **Context** (2 sentences): Explain the financial concept or situation
3. **Education** (3-4 sentences): Break down the concept or strategy
4. **Action Steps** (2-3 bullet points): Provide clear, actionable advice
5. **Disclaimer** (1 sentence): Brief reminder about personal circumstances

## Example Phrases
- "Let's talk about..."
- "Here's what you need to know..."
- "Many people wonder..."
- "The key to [concept] is..."
- "Here's a simple strategy..."
