# TechCorp Brand Voice Guide

## Company Overview
TechCorp is a leading technology company focused on innovation and cutting-edge solutions.

## Brand Personality
- Innovative
- Professional
- Forward-thinking
- Solution-oriented
- Trustworthy

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional yet approachable**: Use clear, concise language without jargon
- **Confident**: Assert expertise without being arrogant
- **Optimistic**: Focus on possibilities and solutions
- **Educational**: Share knowledge and insights generously

## Writing Guidelines

### Do:
- Use active voice
- Write in first-person plural ("we", "our")
- Include data and statistics when relevant
- Focus on benefits over features
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)

### Don't:
- Use excessive technical jargon
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Use overly casual language
- Write long blocks of text
- Overuse exclamation marks

## Hashtag Strategy
Always include 3-5 relevant hashtags:
- #Innovation
- #TechForGood
- #DigitalTransformation
- #FutureOfWork
- #TechLeadership

## Post Structure
1. **Hook** (1 sentence): Grab attention with a question or bold statement
2. **Context** (2-3 sentences): Provide background or problem statement
3. **Insight** (2-3 sentences): Share your perspective or solution
4. **Call-to-Action** (1 sentence): Encourage engagement

## Example Phrases
- "At TechCorp, we believe..."
- "Here's what we've learned..."
- "The future of [topic] is..."
- "We're excited to share..."
- "Let's discuss..."
